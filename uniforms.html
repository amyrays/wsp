School Uniforms

Students at Spring Lake Elementary are required to wear school uniforms. Uniforms are a simple, sensible way to increase pride in our school and increase concentration on classroom activities.

School uniforms do not have ot be purchased from any particular vendor nor is the school insignia required.

Uniforms will also be on sale at the Open House and at designated times throughout the year from vendors.

Please feel free to donate any uniforms that your child has outgrown to the school. Likewise, if your student is in need of a uniform, please contact the school at (407)746-1650.
